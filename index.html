03. My Philosophy

My process is simple and agile - start with user needs and merge those with the business and organisational needs to generate design specifications and KPIs. Design research, user experience workshops and hard data form the basis for all design decisions.

I collaborate closely with digital designers and programmers to explore the possible and strive for the impossible - the perfect UI. Economy and simplicity are the key to creating great UIs. My iterative approach embodies this philosophy to produce measurable results for my clients that continually evolve to fit their business goals and user needs.
